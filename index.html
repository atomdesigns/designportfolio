About Me

I am an energetic, hard driven individual who loves design, art, psychology, and technology. I am passionate about improving the experience in products and services that users use. It is what makes designing more challenging and fun.

Where I am from...

Born and raised at the heart of Los Angeles, Southern California, I recently came back from Boston, MA and explored the city, the food, and the public transportations! I loved the city and the trains a lot! I also loved the great opportunities in UX as I stayed there for 6 months. I went to California State University of Los Angeles and studied Graphic Design | Visual Communications, B.A. I had entered in a M.E.S.A Web Development Competition in high school, won Third place and that's when I got into coding. After I graduated, I went to study web development on my own. I fell in love with UX after my first job in the ecommerce business and I learned a lot from my enthusiastic and amazing mentor Sharon Torres, the former Experience Director of Kelley Blue Book.
